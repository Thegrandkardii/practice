What do we do

MEST is an Africa-wide technology entrepreneur training program, internal seed fund,
and network of hubs offering incubation for technology startups in Africa.

For young aspiring and established African entrepreneurs, MEST Africa provides the skills, mindset, and experiences
to build a globally successful tech company. Beyond tech and entrepreneurial skills training,
MEST Africa provides a real-world advantage to entrepreneurs through funding, business incubation, and access to a global tech network.
